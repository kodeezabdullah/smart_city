About Islamabad Smart City

Your Comprehensive Guide to Civic Facilities

Welcome

Islamabad Smart City WebGIS is a comprehensive Geographic Information System designed to help residents and visitors discover civic facilities across Islamabad, Pakistan. Our platform makes it easy to find hospitals, schools, parks, mosques, police stations, and educational institutions with just a few clicks.

Whether you're looking for the nearest hospital, searching for parks in your sector, or planning a visit to educational institutions, our interactive map and powerful search features have you covered.

Key Features

Smart Search

Natural language search like "mosque near me" or "hospitals in F-7"

Near Me Feature

Find facilities within custom radius (1-10km) from your location

Turn-by-Turn Directions

Get detailed navigation with step-by-step instructions

Favorites System

Save your favorite locations for quick access

Statistics Dashboard

Interactive charts showing facility distribution

Share & Export

Share locations via social media or export to CSV
